Split The Bill

Ever went to a restaurant and they don't allow you to split the bill amongst your friends. Now everyone is scrambling to either find cash or determine who is fronting the bill. Not only that, but how much does each person have to pay? Split The Bill solves all of these problems for you!

With Split The Bill, all of the hard work is automatically done for you. You can create your account and then invite friends to your "table". Each user then adds their plate, along with the price for that plate. The owner of the table can see individual's appetizers, drinks, entrees, and desserts. The creator of the table can track who has settled their share of the bill by marking when their friend has paid their bill. Split The Bill does this efficiently without any manual math from you or your friends.
